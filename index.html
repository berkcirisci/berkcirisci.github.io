Hi. I'm Berk Cirisci.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science from Universite Paris Cite - IRIF, under the supervision of Constantin Enea. I have two Bachelor's Degrees with a double major in Computer Science and Engineering and Electronics Engineering from Sabanci University in Istanbul. Then I obtained a Master's Degree in Computer Science and Engineering (with thesis) again from Sabanci University. My supervisor during my Master's Degree programme was Husnu Yenigun.
